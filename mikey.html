Mikey is a rising freshman in Lawrence's Rider University where he plans to study geosciences. With his natural love for the sciences and dinosaurs, Mikey has always been passionate about paleontology. Feel free to talk to him anytime about your favorite creatures! During his time at Hightstown High School, Mikey was one of the school's most trusted student council members even serving as the student representative for the EWRSD Board of Education. His passion and skill for art also landed him at the head of the school's artist union where he was commissioned for various projects throughout the school. As a talented musician, Mikey has years of experience in the wind ensemble. When he's not at school, he's usually playing video games with his friends or spending time with his family.
